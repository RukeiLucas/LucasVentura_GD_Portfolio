Synopsis

Not far from planet earth, there is a war between alien artificial intelligence units and human soldiers.
Fight for humanity and finish what others couldn't, with your spaceship in the void of space shooting and dodging bullets from high intelligent aliens you are one of the few hopes left to humanity.
StarCore, the Special Force created during this war, initially were only a group of normal human soldiers, but as time went by StarCore started to use body modifications and augmentations, therefore losing some of their humanity in order to keep up against the enemy. After using alien technology and losing an unfortunate great number of soldiers, there is finally a chance for humanity to win.
