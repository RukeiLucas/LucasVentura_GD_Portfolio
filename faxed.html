Synopsis

In a 90s office, there is a fax machine called Chris that after printing thousands of faxes, started thinking about its unpleasant situation and decided to run away.
Play as Chris and avoid the boss and his unpleaseant employees by hiding behind furniture and inside the delivery boxes found around the office, by crouching under their lines of sight or even by avoiding everyone in hope no one sees you
Good luck and get Chris out of there!
